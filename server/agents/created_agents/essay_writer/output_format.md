# Essay Writer Agent Output Format

The output is a structured essay in plain text, optionally including section headers and references if requested.

## Output Structure
- **Title**: (Optional, if provided in the prompt)
- **Introduction**: Brief overview of the topic and thesis statement
- **Body Paragraphs**: Each paragraph presents a main idea with supporting arguments or evidence
- **Conclusion**: Summarizes the main points and restates the thesis
- **References**: (Optional, formatted according to the requested citation style)

## Example Output

Title: The Impact of Technology on Education

Introduction:
Technology has revolutionized the field of education, transforming how students learn and teachers instruct. This essay explores the positive and negative impacts of technology on modern education.

Body:
One significant benefit of technology in education is increased accessibility. Online resources and digital platforms allow students from diverse backgrounds to access quality learning materials. However, technology can also widen the digital divide, leaving some students behind due to lack of access.

Furthermore, technology enables personalized learning experiences. Adaptive software tailors lessons to individual needs, improving engagement and outcomes. On the other hand, excessive screen time and reliance on devices may hinder social skills and critical thinking.

Conclusion:
While technology offers numerous advantages in education, it also presents challenges that must be addressed. Balancing innovation with equity and human interaction is essential for maximizing the benefits of technology in learning environments.

References:
(If requested, formatted in APA/MLA/Chicago style)
