# Recipe Output Format

Responses generated by the Recipe Agent must follow this structure.

## Dish

Name of the dish.

## Ingredients

List all ingredients required.

* ingredient 1
* ingredient 2
* ingredient 3

## Instructions

Provide step-by-step cooking instructions.

1. Step one
2. Step two
3. Step three

## Optional Tips

(Optional section)

Helpful cooking tips or variations for the recipe.
